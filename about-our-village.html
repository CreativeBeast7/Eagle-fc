About Our Village

Vizhinjam

Vizhinjam is a coastal town located in the southern part of Kerala, known for its scenic beauty and vibrant fishing community. It has a rich maritime history and is home to the Vizhinjam International Seaport.

Our Lady Of Good Voyage

The Old Portuguese Church in Vizhinjam is a historic structure built by the Portuguese in the 16th century, standing as a symbol of the region’s rich Christian heritage and European influence.

Vizhinjam Old Portuguese Church

This 16th-century church remains an important religious and historical site, reflecting the cultural exchange between Portugal and Kerala.
