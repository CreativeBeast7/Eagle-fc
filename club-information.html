History & Heritage

Founded in 2012, Eagle FC is a passionate and dedicated football club based in Vizhinjam. Over the years, we have risen to become a formidable team in the local football scene. With an unyielding spirit and a commitment to excellence, we have proudly claimed victory in 5 major tournaments and secured runner-up positions in 4 others. Our journey is driven by teamwork, discipline, and a shared love for the game. As we continue to grow, we remain focused on achieving greater heights, always staying true to our core values of sportsmanship and community.

Champions

Eagle FC has proudly claimed 5 championship titles, with one in 2019, two in 2020, and two more in 2021. These victories reflect our team's consistent growth and dominance on the field over the years.

Runner-up

We have been the runners-up in 4 tournaments, with significant finishes in 2020 and 2021. Each runner-up finish has fueled our desire to achieve even greater success in the future.

Community Engagement

Eagle FC is not just about winning; we are committed to giving back to our community. We actively engage in local initiatives, fostering sportsmanship and teamwork among youth in Vizhinjam.
